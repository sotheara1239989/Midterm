Our Story

From a Small Studio to a Thriving Community

SwiftFit Studio started in 2012 as a single-room personal training space with one coach and a handful of clients. From day one, our founder Marcus Webb had one guiding principle: treat every client like the only client.

Over the years, that philosophy attracted the right people — coaches who cared deeply and clients who were ready to commit. Today, SwiftFit is home to a team of 8 certified trainers and over 500 active members across all fitness levels.

We expanded our facilities in 2019 and launched online coaching in 2021, but our core has never changed — personal, evidence-based, results-driven training built around the individual.

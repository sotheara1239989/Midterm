FAQ

Common Questions

Can I change my plan after signing up?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle with no penalty.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
No contracts and no lock-in periods. All plans are month-to-month and you can cancel anytime with 7 days' notice before your next billing date.
What happens if I miss a session?
Sessions cancelled with 24+ hours notice can be rescheduled at no charge. Late cancellations may be forfeited depending on your plan. Your coach will work with you to find what fits best.
Do you offer online coaching?
Yes. All plans can be delivered fully online via video sessions and our training app. Remote clients receive the same level of coaching as in-person members.
